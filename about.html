About me

Hi, I’m Trine — an outdoor-loving educator with nearly 15 years of experience guiding curious young minds through forests, fields, and the occasional gloriously muddy adventure. My professional foundation is rooted in outdoor education, where fresh air, resilience, and hands-on learning are part of the everyday curriculum.


Somewhere along the trail, my curiosity expanded beyond the treetops and into the digital landscape. I developed a strong interest in cybersecurity — particularly how we can protect our digital lives, both personally and professionally. That curiosity led me back to university, where I am now deepening my knowledge in cybersecurity and digital safety.


When I’m not working or studying, you’ll most likely find me outside — hiking in the mountains, paddling along the coast, or exploring with a camera in hand. I’m a hobby photographer with a passion for natural light and quiet landscapes. No elaborate gear — just patience, perspective, and an appreciation for the details nature offers.


In addition to my work and studies, I also run a small wellness business as a Forever Business Owner. Through Forever Living, I offer a range of Aloe‑vera–based health and skincare products developed by a company with long‑standing expertise in cultivating and refining high‑quality natural ingredients. It’s a meaningful extension of my interest in balanced living and supporting others in their everyday wellbeing.
